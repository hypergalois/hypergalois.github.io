DNSSEC is a good example of why cryptographic migration is never only about
cryptography.

When signatures and keys grow, the effects appear in packet size, truncation,
fragmentation, resolver choices, retries, and operational defaults. These are
systems behaviors, not footnotes.

## Boundary tests

The useful testbed changes one dimension at a time and records how the system
responds. The result should be boring, repeatable, and easy to compare.

That is the style of artifact a portfolio should highlight: not just an idea, but
a way to make the idea testable.
