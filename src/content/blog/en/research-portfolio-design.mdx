A strong technical portfolio should not feel like a landing page trying to close
a sale. It should feel like an artifact index.

The homepage gives orientation. Projects provide evidence. Writing shows taste.
The profile page explains how to read the work. Everything else should get out of
the way without becoming lifeless.

## Useful constraints

- Keep the site fast
- Make categories visible
- Give projects enough structure to be evaluated
- Avoid decorative complexity that does not improve reading
- Let technical detail carry status

That is the line this site is designed to walk.
