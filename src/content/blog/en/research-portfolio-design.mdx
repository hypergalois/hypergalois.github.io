A technical portfolio works best as an artifact index.

The homepage gives orientation. Projects carry evidence. Writing records technical
choices. The profile page gives the reading frame.

## Useful constraints

- Fast pages
- Visible categories
- Project pages with question, method, artifact, and scope
- Visual structure tied to reading and comparison
- Technical detail as the main status signal

That structure keeps the work inspectable from the first page.
