## Abstract

Post-quantum TLS migration changes deployed protocol behavior across handshake
size, certificate-chain composition, fallback paths, client support, and
middlebox behavior. This manuscript frames migration evidence as a measurement
object: traces, normalized observations, static reports, and scope notes.

## Record

- Object: deployed TLS behavior under post-quantum migration pressure.
- Method: endpoint probing, trace capture, chain inspection, and normalized reports.
- Evidence: comparable runs, raw traces, summarized outputs, and failure labels.
- Scope: measurement design and artifact structure for research review.
