## Abstract

Automated research workflows need reviewable evidence: scenario inputs, tool
calls, intermediate decisions, touched files, failure labels, recovery behavior,
and final outputs. This manuscript outline treats agent runs as experimental
records that support comparison across systems and prompts.

## Record

- Object: automated research work across tool-using agents.
- Method: scenario design, trace capture, failure labeling, and human review.
- Evidence: tool-call logs, diffs, outputs, and review records.
- Scope: evaluation scaffolding for research engineering workflows.
