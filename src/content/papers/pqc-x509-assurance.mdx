## Abstract

Post-quantum certificate material changes certificate size, parser stress,
path-building behavior, transport limits, and validation assumptions. This
manuscript organizes assurance around reproducible cases: corpus entries,
validator decisions, parser behavior, and lint reports.

## Methodology

The harness builds certificate-path cases with classical, hybrid, and
post-quantum material. Each case is parsed, linted, minimized where useful, and
checked against path-validation behavior. The paper records which assumptions
move when certificate size, algorithm identifiers, and chain composition change.

## Results

The current results isolate assurance boundaries for post-quantum X.509:
parser stress, transport limits, path-building assumptions, and regression
cases that should remain visible during migration.
