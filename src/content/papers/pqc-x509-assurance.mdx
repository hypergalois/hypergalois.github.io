## Abstract

Post-quantum certificate material changes certificate size, parser stress,
path-building behavior, transport limits, and validation assumptions. This
manuscript organizes assurance around reproducible cases: corpus entries,
validator decisions, parser behavior, and lint reports.

## Record

- Object: X.509 certificate paths under post-quantum structures.
- Method: parser tests, path validation experiments, and regression cases.
- Evidence: lint reports, validator outputs, and minimized examples.
- Scope: certificate assurance for migration analysis and tooling review.
