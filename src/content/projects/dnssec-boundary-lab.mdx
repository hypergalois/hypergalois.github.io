## Problem

DNSSEC deployments already live near operational boundaries. Larger signatures
and keys make those boundaries more visible.

## Technical record

The lab is organized around controlled experiments: change one dimension, capture
the behavior, and compare resolver and network responses under repeatable
conditions.

## Operational scope

The lab records packet size, truncation, fragmentation, resolver choices, retries,
and network responses under controlled input changes.
