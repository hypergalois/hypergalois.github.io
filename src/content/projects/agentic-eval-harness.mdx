## Problem

Agentic tooling can look impressive while hiding the hard questions: observed
state, changed files, failures, and reproducibility status.

## Technical record

The harness treats automated work as an experiment. It records scenarios, tool
calls, outputs, failures, and review notes so comparisons are inspectable rather
than anecdotal.

## Evidence

Evidence trails expose quality, regressions, and failure modes across runs.
