## Problem

Agentic tooling can look impressive while hiding the hard questions: what was
observed, what changed, what failed, and whether the result can be reproduced.

## Artifact

The harness treats automated work as an experiment. It records scenarios, tool
calls, outputs, failures, and review notes so comparisons are inspectable rather
than anecdotal.

## Why it matters

Research automation needs more than demos. It needs evidence trails that make
quality, regressions, and failure modes visible.
