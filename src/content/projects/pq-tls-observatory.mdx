## Problem

Post-quantum migration is not just an algorithm swap. Real deployments expose
failure modes in handshake size, certificate chains, middleboxes, fallback paths,
and operational defaults.

## Artifact

This project is framed as an observatory: a repeatable measurement pipeline that
collects protocol evidence, normalizes it, and turns deployment behavior into
inspectable data.

## Why it matters

The goal is not a dashboard for its own sake. The goal is to make the migration
surface visible enough that research claims and engineering decisions can be
checked against real traces.

## Stack

- TLS probing and packet capture
- Certificate-chain inspection
- Reproducible experiment runners
- Static reporting for long-lived evidence
